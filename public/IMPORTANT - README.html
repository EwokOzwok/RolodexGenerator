üëã Hey there! First time using the command line? No worries at all! This might look a bit technical at first, but we promise it's way easier than it seems. Think of it like following a recipe - just take it one step at a time, and you'll have your app running in about 10 minutes. You've got this! üí™

1Get Your Data File Ready

Find your CSV file (the spreadsheet with all your data) and rename it to exactly rolodex.csv

Put this file in the same folder as all your other Rolodex App files.

üí° Tip: Make sure the CSV has the same columns as the original file you used.

2Download Docker Desktop

Docker is like a self-contained box that has everything your app needs to run. Don't worry about what it does behind the scenes - just think of it as the engine that powers your app!

Download it here: Docker Desktop

Choose the Mac or Windows version depending on your computer.

3Install Docker Desktop

Double-click the file you just downloaded and follow the installation steps (it's just like installing any other program - keep clicking "Next" or "Continue").

After it installs, open Docker Desktop and wait for it to start up completely. You'll see a little whale icon when it's ready.

4Open Your Command Window

ü™ü For Windows Users:

  1. Click the Start menu (Windows logo in the bottom-left corner)
  2. Type powershell
  3. Click on Windows PowerShell when it appears
  4. A blue window with white text will open - this is where you'll type commands!

üçé For Mac Users:

  1. Press Command + Space on your keyboard
  2. Type terminal
  3. Press Enter
  4. A white or black window will open - this is where you'll type commands!

5Navigate to Your App Folder

Now you need to tell your computer where your Rolodex App files are. We'll use the cd command (which stands for "change directory" - but just think of it as "go to this folder").

Here's how:

  1. Find your Rolodex App folder on your computer (maybe it's on your Desktop or in Documents)
  2. Right-click on the folder and look for an option like "Copy as path" (Windows) or hold Option and right-click to see "Copy as Pathname" (Mac)
  3. Go back to your PowerShell/Terminal window
  4. Type cd (with a space after it), then paste the path you copied
  5. Press Enter

Example for Windows:

cd "C:\Users\YourName\Desktop\Rolodex App"

Example for Mac:

cd "/Users/YourName/Desktop/Rolodex App"
üí° Pro tip: On Mac, you can also type cd (with a space) and then drag the folder directly into the Terminal window - it'll automatically paste the path!

6Build and Start Your App

Almost there! Now we'll create and start your app with two simple commands. Just copy and paste these one at a time:

First command (this builds your app - might take 2-3 minutes):

docker build -f Dockerfile.txt -t rolodex .

Wait for it to finish (you'll see lots of text scrolling by - that's normal!), then run the second command (this starts your app):

docker run -d --restart=always -p 1222:1222 --name RolodexApp rolodex
üéâ That's it! Your app is now running on your computer!

7Open Your App in a Browser

Open your favorite web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.) and go to:

http://localhost:1222

You should see your Rolodex App! üéä

üí° Bookmark it! As long as Docker Desktop is running, you can always get back to your app at this address.

üåê Want to Share Your App Online for FREE? (Recommended!)

Right now, your app only works on your computer. Want to share it with others so they can access it from anywhere? The easiest way is using shinyapps.io - it's free and surprisingly simple!

Step 1: Create Your Free Account

Go to shinyapps.io and sign up for a free account. It takes about 30 seconds!

Step 2: Download and Install R

R is the programming language your app uses. Don't worry - you won't need to write any code!

  • For Windows: Download R for Windows
  • For Mac: Download R for Mac

Run the installer and follow the prompts (just keep clicking "Next" or "Continue").

Step 3: Download and Install RStudio

RStudio is like a friendly control panel for R. It makes everything visual and easy!

Download RStudio Desktop (Free version): Get RStudio

Install it just like any other program.

Step 4: Open Your App in RStudio

  1. Open RStudio (not R - they're different!)
  2. Click File ‚Üí Open File... from the top menu
  3. Find your Rolodex App folder and open the file called app.R

Step 5: Connect RStudio to Your shinyapps.io Account

You'll need to connect RStudio to your shinyapps.io account just once. Here's how:

  1. Go to your shinyapps.io dashboard
  2. Click on your name in the top-right corner, then select Tokens
  3. Click Show next to "Token" and then Show Secret
  4. Click Copy to clipboard
  5. Back in RStudio, paste this into the Console (the bottom-left panel) and press Enter
üí° Need more detailed help? Check out this complete step-by-step guide: Deploying to shinyapps.io

Step 6: Publish Your App! üöÄ

This is the exciting part - and it's super easy!

  1. Make sure your app.R file is open in RStudio
  2. Look for a blue "Publish" button in the top-right corner of the code editor
  3. Click it!
  4. A window will pop up - just click Publish again
  5. Wait a minute or two while your app uploads (you'll see a progress bar)
üéâ Done! Your app is now live on the internet! You'll get a link that looks like https://yourname.shinyapps.io/rolodex/ that you can share with anyone!
